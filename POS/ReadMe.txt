Font:
Unzip the folder, and install the font. In VS form, click on the blank white rectangle white box that is below the cart and above the print receipt button. Press it to reveal properties (or just right click and press properties). Scroll till you find the font section then press the ... to change it.
Then when selecting font just search for the font and select it. If you get a font error just 
restart visual studio.
